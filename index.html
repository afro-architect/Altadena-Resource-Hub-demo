Altadena Rebuild

A Resource Hub for Residents experiencing loss from the wildfires

Welcome!

Discover the Altadena Resource Hub—your one-stop center for community support, connection, and empowerment. The Hub was created to bring together the services, resources, and information that Altadena residents need most. Whether you’re looking for assistance, want to get involved, or simply want to stay in the loop, the Hub is here for you. Our mission is to strengthen our community by making help accessible and building a network of support for everyone.

At the Altadena Resource Hub, we believe that everyone deserves access to the tools and support they need to thrive. From food assistance and housing resources to educational programs and wellness workshops, our goal is to connect you with the help you need, when you need it.

We’re also a place for connection! Meet your neighbors, share your story, and become part of a caring, active community that’s always ready to lend a hand.
